# Enterprise Cloud Infrastructure and Managed Services RFP

## 1. Technical and Compliance Requirements

### Clause 1.1 (C1) – Uptime Guarantee
The Bidder must provide a managed cloud platform with a minimum annual service availability of **99.9% uptime**, excluding scheduled maintenance windows that do not exceed four (4) hours per month. The proposal must include the technical architecture or operational controls used to achieve this service level.

### Clause 1.2 (C2) – Customer Support
The Bidder shall provide **outstanding customer support and fast responses** throughout the contract period. The proposal should describe the support approach, communication channels, and customer engagement model.

### Clause 1.3 (C3) – Information Security Compliance
The Bidder must comply with **ISO/IEC 27001** information security requirements and demonstrate that all customer data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption. Documentary evidence of compliance shall be included in the proposal.
